About

Leather is made up of native materials especially in the Philippines. Due to the good quality of materials its, very affordable and highest rating, its no surprise than the leather industry generates an annual revenue of more than Fifty-three billion. This is surprisingly higher than the revenues of coffee, tea, rice, rubber, cotton and sugar combined. Leather shoes take the top spot for generating more precent of the revenue. Due to the standard of it, people often confuse top grain leather with being the most genuine and highest quality leather you can buy when in fact, full grain is considered the highest quality leather available on the market. In the last century, it was all the fashion to have your house wallpapered with leather in places such as Barcelona in Italy.
